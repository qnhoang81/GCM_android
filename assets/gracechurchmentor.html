Doing Church God's Way

Grace Church of Mentor is committed to doing church God's Way, and we are certain you will notice a difference.

Who We Are

Family in God’s Word

We are a church family faithful to the Word of God. Like many healthy families, you will see us spending time together at events like a church picnic or in small group bible studies. We make time for each other’s personal events—to rejoice in times of celebration or support in times of grief. Most of all, we look forward to worship when we hear God’s Word, sing and pray together.

Event Calendar | Faithfulness to God’s Word

Personal in Discipleship

A relationship with Jesus Christ is personal. There is no reason why church relationships should not be personal as well. Every believer should be discipled by a mentor and every disciple should be mentoring. Jesus modeled this discipleship ministry. We follow His model and are intentional about being and making disciples.

Discipleship at GCM | A Relationship with Christ

Progressing in Faith

We have a culture of growth. You will find Grace to be an exciting place to learn, build and live your faith. Weekly worship services, bible studies, formal classroom training, and a variety of children, teen and adult ministries provide the venue to grow, demonstrate and share our faith.

Our Senior Pastor

Pastor Tim Potter

Pastor Tim Potter loves God's Word and loves God's people. His goal is to preach and teach the Word of God with such clarity and personal application that Grace Church of Mentor will be "conformed to the image of Jesus Christ."

He believes with firm, loving conviction that the best way to influence our culture is to bring souls to Jesus Christ by maintaining a solid personal testimony and witness, teaching and preaching the Word of God with passion, and discipling new believers with the Word of God.
